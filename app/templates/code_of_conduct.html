Code of Conduct

Our goal is to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and safe environment for all members of the campus community.

Respect and Inclusivity

  • Treat everyone with respect regardless of background, identity, or role.
  • Do not engage in harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.

Privacy and Security

  • Do not share personal information of others without their consent.
  • Report any suspicious or inappropriate content immediately.

Responsible Use

  • Only post legitimate lost or found items.
  • Do not use the platform for spam or unrelated content.

Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in removal of content, account suspension, or other actions deemed necessary by the administrators.

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the site admin immediately.
